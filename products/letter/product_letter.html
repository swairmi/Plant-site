Redwood Large Letter Kit

$139.00


These rustic wood and sphagnum moss succulent frames are made by a california artist from reclaimed redwood that is repurposed into unique succulent planters. The Redwood Large Letter Kit contains the frame, and an informational postcard containing the directions you need to plant your perfect living letter.

Please note that the cuttings included are a mix comprised mostly of sempervivums with a few accent cuts. This mix is always in flux and changes depending on what we are cutting. Sempervivums take time to color up and as they root and mature, the small sempervivums will open and expand

These frames are custom made to order, so please allow 3-4 weeks for delivery

Large Letters adverage 19"
