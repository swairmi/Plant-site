Our Story

We are Audrey and Min

Phoenix is a creative plant stuido that has put together a unique collection of handmade terrariums and potted plants. Behind each creation are desginers Audrey and Min, with over 30 years of combined experience within the floral industry

Audrey and Min illustrate their odern take to natural design, incorporating playfuless and sincerity into their worlds. These small gardens live inside quality glass vessels, featuring a cluster of plants, mosses, shells & other natural wonders.
